The Best Movies According to Durim

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Shawshank Redemtion

This movie is a story of a man in a prison on how he escapes only using a small hammer to scrape a hole in a wall. He and his friends that he made in the prison tell the story of how people in prisons live day by day and show their human side even though some of them did something really bad.

Fast & Furious

This movie is a series type of movie. It has 10 parts and is a movie that tells a story about a group that are passionate about cars and riding them with speed and drifting through very different courses. Also how they stay close and together when things get tough.

Outer Banks

This is a series where a group of teenagers live in a small town called Outer Banks. It's a town that is sorrounded by water and they use the water to surf. Some of them teenagers are poor and they have a mission to find this gold that older people were trying to find for generations.
